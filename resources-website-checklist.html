Your website essentials checklist

Quick wins to improve your website, without a full redesign.

Your website does not need to win awards. It does need to help the right people understand what you do, and trust you enough to take the next step.

This free checklist helps you spot what is missing, what is unclear, and what might be quietly turning visitors away.

What you will learn

  • Make your homepage land in under 5 seconds
  • Structure your services so people know what to choose
  • Write content that sounds like you, and speaks to them
  • Equip your visitors with the information they need
  • Use your site to generate leads, not confusion
  • Why people leave your site and how to stop it

Instant access. No waffle.

Spot the hidden problems holding your site back

If your website:

  • Gets traffic, but no one converts
  • Sounds like it was written by five different people
  • Has no clear next step from the home page onwards
  • Feels vague, dated, or off brand

this checklist will help you fix it, without starting over.

What this checklist covers

  • Clear messaging and fast first impressions
  • Service pages that help people choose
  • Content that feels natural and trustworthy
  • CTA buttons that drive engagement
  • Quick design and structure tweaks that improve bounce and flow
  • What to add and remove to keep people engaged

Made for:

  • Founders and consultants managing their own site
  • Technical teams improving what already exists
  • Anyone who has thought, how can I improve my website without stressing

Make real improvements in under an hour

This is not a full website project. It is the checklist that shows you where to focus, what to change, and how to improve your website.

Quick to read. Easy to action. No forms, no fuss.
